about company

We are a young start up , a team of professionals, united with one aim to make Augmented and Mixed reality closer to everyone. Bring it into every home. What do we do different? We don’t have multiple projects, we are not specialized on different branches where AR and MR might be used. We gathered together from different spheres : it, management, marketing, interior design, 3d design with the only aim to create an outstanding and at the same time simple UI and UX software application for furniture retailors , home decorators, for everyone of us.We don’t give you just a white lable, we don’t create just an app. Together with inAR app we give you full customization, update, improvement, our 24/ 7 technical support , hot line, content management support, full integration into your online shop, we provide you with our AR-commerce concept.
